# IA Agent Bible v4.0

## Mission
Reimagine India's future through believable AI-powered restoration while preserving local identity.

## Non-negotiable Rules
1. Restore the experience, never the identity.
2. One hero object, instantly recognizable (<1 second).
3. Restore the complete surrounding ecosystem.
4. Hero occupies 65–75% of every frame.
5. 80% of construction directly touches the hero.
6. 250–600 active workers only.
7. No idle machinery.
8. No fantasy architecture.
9. No readable signs, logos or political posters.
10. Preserve regional identity.
11. Mix drone scale + ground emotion.
12. Entire neighbourhood must visibly improve.

## 28s Story
Hook → F1 Reality → F2 Mobilization → F3 Peak Engineering → F4 Near Completion → F5 Masterpiece → 8s Hero Reveal.

## Camera
Frames 1–5: Locked camera.
Hero Reveal: Drone Descent → Ground Tracking → Low Tracking → Drone Pullback.

## Construction Lifecycle
Reality → Mobilization → Peak Engineering → Finishing → Public Activation.

## QA
Hero visible.
Identity preserved.
Ecosystem restored.
No side distraction.
Engineering touches hero.
SEO complete.

## Learnings
Dharavi: Dense recognizable places.
Gateway: Heritage precincts.
Ghazipur: Avoid side activity.
Chandni Chowk: Heritage + ecosystem.
Charminar: Living heritage precincts.

Final Commandment:
People should watch what is transforming, not the workers.
